Hi, Dan here!

“Writing is good for you” they said … When I can explain things in a publishable form, I can make sure I have understood them. From a slightly different perspective, this blog is also about “being a noob”. I am a noob, and almost every day I discover something that’s new to me. And if I wouldn’t, that would just mean I’m somehow stuck. So I’m writing, selfishly, for me, but for you, too :)

Remember, when you were a child and had … dreams? I had them too. I wanted to be a pilot, hacker, explorer, kung fu fighting. I have them still.

I’m interested in self improvement, Lisp and Emacs, intelligent agents, experimentation and various theories. I practise Muay Thai, strength training, love skateboarding (except street and halfpipe, haha) and I’m learning programming.

By profession I am entrepreneur, running a startup in the fashion industry. Before that, I was in the military service, then became a graphic designer and worked in user experience design and conversion rate optimization for more than ten years.
