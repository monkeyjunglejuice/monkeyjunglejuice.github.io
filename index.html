Bio

Hi, Dan here! Welcome to my personal blog, where I track some aspects of my journey and share the findings along the way. I’ve got a love for examining phenomena from various perspectives and a deep fascination with things ’bottom-up’.

I’m a designer, entrepreneur, and co-founder of a footwear brand. Before embracing entrepreneurship, I specialized in conversion rate optimization, a field I found thrilling for its empirical approach to dissecting human decision-making and persuasion.

When I’m not sitting on my butt — which has become a more common occurrence recently — I’m throwing elbows and knees in Muay Thai, shredding the streets on my old-school skateboard, taking strolls along the beach, or listening to the wind in the leaves.
